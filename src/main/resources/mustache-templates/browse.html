This SMART on FHIR application demonstrates the ability to find and report suspected phenotypes for a patient based on clinical data retrieved from a FHIR server. Patient lab results are retrieved and converted to terms in the Human Phenotype Ontology. The phenotypes can be appended to the patient record in FHIR for analysis and diagnostics.
